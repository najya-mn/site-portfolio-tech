Graphic Design

Redesigned PPDKL's directional signage to be manually rotatable, enabling a single sign to serve both left and right directions.

This cost-effective solution features text printed on the top and bottom of the arrow line, ensuring that the text remains upright and readable when the sign is rotated by the user.

This design simplifies wayfinding and reduces printing expenses.

Redesigned an outdated business card as an intern project, under the guidance of my coworker, to create a more modern and professional look.

Key changes included a cleaner sans-serif font for improved readability and a more balanced layout with clearer hierarchy through the use of font sizes and visual spacing.

This experience provided valuable lessons in typography and layout.
